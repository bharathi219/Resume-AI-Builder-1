Job Title: Data Scientist

We are looking for a Data Scientist to join our growing analytics team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in statistics, machine learning, and data analysis.

Responsibilities:
- Develop predictive models and machine learning algorithms.
- Analyze large volumes of structured and unstructured data.
- Communicate findings to stakeholders and make recommendations.
- Collaborate with engineering teams to deploy models.

Required Skills:
- Strong programming skills in Python and SQL
- Experience with libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Understanding of machine learning, deep learning, and NLP techniques
- Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Good communication and analytical skills

Qualifications:
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field
- 1-3 years of experience in a similar role
