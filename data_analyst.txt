Job Title: Data Analyst

The Data Analyst will gather, process, and analyze large datasets to help the company make data-driven decisions.

Required Skills:
- SQL
- Excel
- Power BI or Tableau
- Python (Pandas, Numpy)
- Data Cleaning

Qualifications:
- B.Sc. or M.Sc. in Statistics, Computer Science, or related field.
- At least 1 year of experience in analytics or business intelligence.
